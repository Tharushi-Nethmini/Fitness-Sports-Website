Mixed Martial Arts

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) is a versatile combat sport combining techniques from boxing, kickboxing, wrestling, and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, gaining global popularity for its exciting, well-rounded competition.

Gym

A gym is a physical exercise facility with exercise machines, weights, and other tools for strength training, cardiovascular exercises, and overall fitness routines. It caters to varying fitness levels, offering structured workouts and professional guidance.

Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport where two participants compete to score points by landing punches on their opponents' bodies and heads. Boxers wear padded gloves and follow rules. Matches usually take place in a square ring, with bouts determined by knockout or other criteria.
